# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Team leads, managers, and KPI owners who need to review performance records, update actual results, compare status across teams, and keep SMART goal details consistent during review cycles.

## Product Purpose

SmartKPI helps teams maintain shared KPI records, track progress against targets, filter operational views, import and export data, and document mid-year and year-end evaluation context. Success means users can quickly spot at-risk KPIs, understand why a rating was assigned, and update records without losing confidence in the data.

## Brand Personality

Clear, accountable, and calm. The interface should feel like a reliable workbench for performance review rather than a decorative report.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style dashboards, oversized decorative metrics, novelty controls, vague productivity copy, and dense forms that hide required decisions. Avoid interfaces that make status colors feel decorative instead of operational.

## Design Principles

1. Prioritize scanning: KPI status, owner, rating, and progress should be readable at a glance.
2. Preserve trust: every edit, delete, import, and sync state should clearly communicate what will happen.
3. Keep controls familiar: use standard tables, filters, buttons, dialogs, and form states so users stay in the task.
4. Make exceptions visible: at-risk records, rating overrides, missing SMART details, and sync errors deserve clear handling.
5. Support review cadence: mid-year and year-end comments should feel like part of the workflow, not leftover notes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain visible focus states, 44px touch targets on mobile, readable contrast for status text, keyboard access for dialogs and table actions, and reduced-motion alternatives for UI transitions.
